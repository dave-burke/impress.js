Mission statement

To build an active, independent, technology-focused community of enthusiasts, skill collectors, and makers where members can propose or join fun and interesting projects to promote collaborative continuous learning and self-improvement.

Core values

  • The primary motivations for club activities are curiosity and fun.
  • People of all skill levels are welcome. All members and ideas will be treated with respect.
  • Members will have opportunities to collaborate and build friendships.
